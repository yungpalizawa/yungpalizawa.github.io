Description

The sweetest prepared form of plantain

Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel the Plantain and cut into small sizes
  2. Pour salt into the peeled and cut plantain,then mix
  3. Pour Groundnut Oil in a Frying Pan
  4. Drop the plantain into the frying pan the fry
